# Signs in RapidPath

RapidPath does not include country signs addons by default. Sign-related tools are available in RapidPath only when a signs addon is assigned to your license.

If your RapidPath license has a signs addon, use the RapidPlan signs documentation for sign workflows:

- [The Signs Palette](/category/the-signs-palette)
- [Creating Your Own Signs](/category/creating-your-own-signs)

If you need to add signs functionality to RapidPath, contact Invarion sales or manage addons through your Invarion account.
