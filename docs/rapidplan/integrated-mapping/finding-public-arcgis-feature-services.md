## Finding Public ArcGIS Feature Services

RapidPlan can connect directly to ArcGIS Feature Services via the Import Spatial Data tool. This allows accessing transportation, road, traffic, and infrastructure datasets published on ArcGIS Online and ArcGIS Hub portals, as well as servers hosted by many government agencies and public organizations publish.

This page lists locations where you can find compatible public ArcGIS services that can be useful for traffic control planning.

> **Tip:** In addition to the sources below, check whether your federal, state/provincial, regional, or local transportation authorities maintain public ArcGIS services covering your area.

### Australia & New Zealand (ANZ)

| Source | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| Waka Kotahi NZTA Open Data Hub | https://opendata-nzta.opendata.arcgis.com/ — New Zealand national transport datasets, road network, traffic, and infrastructure. |
| NZTA ArcGIS Portal | https://spatial.nzta.govt.nz/portal/home/ — Search for published Feature Services and datasets. |
| Esri Australia Hub | https://www.esriaustraliahub.com.au/ — Aggregates datasets from Australian organizations; filter results to Feature Services where required. |
| Australian Government Spatial Infrastructure Services | https://spatial.infrastructure.gov.au/server/rest/services/Hosted — Contains transportation-related Feature Services including roadworks, traffic counts, rail, ports, and freight data. |
| Vicmap Transport (Victoria) | https://services-ap1.arcgis.com/P744lA0wf4LlBZ84/arcgis/rest/services/Vicmap_Transport/FeatureServer — Road network and transport infrastructure Feature Service. |

### United States & Canada

| Source | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| ArcGIS Hub (Transportation category) | https://hub.arcgis.com/ — Search for transportation datasets and filter results to Feature Services. |
| National Transportation Atlas Database (NTAD) | https://www.bts.gov/ntad — Major roads, rail, transit, freight, and other transportation datasets. |
| North American Roads Feature Service | https://services.arcgis.com/xOi1kZaI0eWDREZv/ArcGIS/rest/services/NTAD_North_American_Roads/FeatureServer — Road network data covering the US, Canada, and Mexico. |
| U.S. Department of Transportation Open Data Portal | https://data.transportation.gov/ — Large catalog of transportation datasets; some records expose ArcGIS Feature Services. |
| Esri Living Atlas | https://livingatlas.arcgis.com/ — Search for authoritative transportation Feature Layers and services published by Esri and partner agencies. |

### Europe

| Source | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| ArcGIS Hub | https://hub.arcgis.com/ — Many European national and regional agencies publish ArcGIS Feature Services through Hub sites. |
| European Data Portal | https://data.europa.eu/ — Search for ArcGIS-hosted datasets; some records provide Feature Service endpoints. |
| INSPIRE Geoportal | https://inspire-geoportal.ec.europa.eu/ — Discover transport and infrastructure datasets published by European public authorities. |
| National ArcGIS Open Data Portals | Many European countries maintain ArcGIS Hub sites (for example national mapping, transport, or road authorities). Search for "`<country>` ArcGIS Hub" or "`<authority>` open data ArcGIS". |
| Local/Regional Government ArcGIS Hubs | Major cities and regions frequently publish road, traffic, parking, and asset datasets through ArcGIS Online and ArcGIS Hub portals. |

### Notes

- When browsing ArcGIS Hub or ArcGIS Online portals, look specifically for **Feature Services** or **Feature Layers**.
- Some portals contain multiple service types (Map Services, Feature Services, WMS, etc.). If so, filter search results to **Feature Services** before copying the service URL into RapidPlan.
- Transportation-related datasets commonly include roads, traffic counts, roadworks, incidents, speed data, rail infrastructure, transit assets, and administrative boundaries.
