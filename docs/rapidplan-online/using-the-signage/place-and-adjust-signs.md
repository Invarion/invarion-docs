# Place and adjust signs

## Place signs

After you place a sign, you can move, resize, and rotate it.

**To place a sign:**

- Select the desired sign from the **Signs palette**.

- Move the sign onto the canvas with the mouse. You do not have to hold the mouse button while you drag the sign.

- Click to place the sign at the selected position.

**Note:** For details about the palette layout, see [Signs palette](/rapidplan-online/rapidplan-online-workspace/signs-palette).

## Adjust signs

Once a sign is placed on the canvas, there are a couple of ways you can adjust it.

### Flip by 90-degree increments

Right-click the selected sign, open **Flip**, then select **Horizontally** or **Vertically**.

### Rotate freely

The orange handles on the exterior of the sign let you adjust the sign orientation. Left-click and hold a handle to move the sign to any angle.

### Resize signs

The yellow handles on the outside of the sign let you adjust the sign size. Left-click and drag a handle to the required size. To resize the sign uniformly, hold **Ctrl** while resizing.
